I am a PhD student at Mila and University of Montreal supervised by Sarath Chandar. I also closely work with Yoshua Bengio and Aditya Mahajan. My main focus is to study the fundamental principles of human intelligence by creating agents that can adapt to new situations and work with different partners, with the ultimate goal of creating cooperative intelligence. Currently, I am specifically researching Cooperative Multi-agent, Offline, Model-based Reinforcement Learning and Lifelong Learning.

I obtained my MSc from Univeristy of Waterloo working with Prof. Khandani . Before that, I received my BSc in Electrical Engineering and a minor degree in Economics at the Sharif University of Technology, Iran.
