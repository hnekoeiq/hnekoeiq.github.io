I am a PhD student at MILA and University of Montreal working with Sarath Chandar and Harm van Seijen. I am also collaborating with Aditya Mahajan. My current research focuses more on Multi-agent Reinforcement Learning, Lifelong Learning, and Model-based RL. In general, I am interested in understanding the principles of human learning by designing agents capable of generalizing to new scenarios and with new partners.

I obtained my MSc from Univeristy of Waterloo working with Prof. Khandani . Before that, I received my BSc in Electrical Engineering and a minor degree in Economics at the Sharif University of Technology, Iran. I was fortunate enough to spend the summer of 2016 working with Prof. Chin Keong Ho at the Institute for Infocom Research, Singapore, generously supported by the Astar Scholarship .
